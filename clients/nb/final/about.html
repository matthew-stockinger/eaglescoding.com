What Is North Berkeley?

North Berkeley is a structural engineering consulting firm. Its sole proprietor is Joel Alexander Hampson, based in Vancouver, BC, Canada.

Joel specialises in natural disaster response and evaluation of buildings in various states of collapse.


The North Berkeley Advantage

  • Quick mobilisation
  • Extensive experience with catastrophic disasters
  • Ability to operate in austere environments
  • Retained by municipalities, adjusters, and NGOs
