North Berkeley

Who is North Berkeley?

North Berkeley are structural-engineering consultants who specialise in responding to natural disasters. We evaluate buildings in various states of collapse. We are prepared to quickly mobilise. We can provide guidance to shore, repair and restore buildings. We have a long history of work after catastrophic earthquakes and hurricanes. We have been retained by municipalities, adjusters and non-governmental organizations.
