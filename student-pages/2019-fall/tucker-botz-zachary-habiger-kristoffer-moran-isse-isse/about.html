What Is North Berkeley?

-North Berkeley are structural-engineering consultants who specialise in responding to natural disasters. We evaluate buildings in various states of collapse. We are prepared to quickly mobilise. We can provide guidance to shore, repair and restore buildings. We have a long history of work after catastrophic earthquakes and hurricanes. We have been retained by municipalities, adjusters and non-governmental organizations.

We will travel anywhere if you need help. We also have a strong team of structural-engineering and will help clean to hopefully speed up the process of cleaning your home and getting you back faster. While we plan how to rebuild or fix the building we try to look for ways to make more stable and wtihstand more then it was able to before. We are quick to respond to you reaching out to us and will be there as soon as possible.
