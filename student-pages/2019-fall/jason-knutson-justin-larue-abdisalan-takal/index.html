North Berkeley:

North Berkeley are structural-engineering consultants who specialise in responding to natural disasters. We evaluate buildings in various states of collapse. We are prepared to quickly mobilize. We can provide guidance to shore, repair and restore buildings. We have a long history of work after catastrophic earthquakes and hurricanes. We have been retained by municipalities, adjusters and non-governmental organizations.

Where we go:

North Berkeley is available globally to help those in need. Some of our missions have been in Asia and Haiti. North Berkeley has the proper equipments to get the job done well.
