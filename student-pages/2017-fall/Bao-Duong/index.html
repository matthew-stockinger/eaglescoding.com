DRAVEN (League Of Legends)

Draven's story

Unlike his brother Darius, Draven always wants to win. He wants fame and glory

He's first area that he tried to seek greatness in was the Noxian military, but he was underappreciated.

He finally got the fame he wanted at a prison where he would execute people and fight off guards for his fame. Everybody wanted to see him fight

Darius is Draven's friend and rival
