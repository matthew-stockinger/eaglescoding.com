Gear

You could have all the skills in the world, but if you don't have the right equipment you will never be an excellent theif. Finding-or creating-the right armor and weapons with enchanments will give you the extra boost in your thieving. Here's a list of the most well known items to look for.

Daggers and Swords

Name Damage Location Enchantment
Mehrune's Razor 11 Silus' Museum Chance for an instant kill.
Chillrend 15 Riftweald Manor 30 frost damage; Chance to paralize.
Nightingale Blade 14 Karliah Absorb 25 health and stamina.
Blade of Woe 12 Astrid Absorb 10 health.
Windshear 11 The Katariah Staggers opponent with every hit.

Armor Sets

Name Defense Location Enchantment
Ancient Shrouded Armor 72 Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary +25 armor with full set; Immune to poison; Muffle; +35% Bow damage; 2x sneak attack with one handed weapons
Nightingale Armor 69 Nightingale Hall +25 Armor with full set; +20 stamina; +15% Resist Frost; Muffle; + 15% Lockpicking; +15% One handed damage; -12% Illusion spell cost.
Deathbrand Armor 78.5 Solstheim +100 armor with full set; +15 stamina, +10 carrying capacity, and +10% damage for one handed weapons per peice of Dragonband armor worn; Waterbreathing.

Always remember that while you can find thousands of armor pieces, a majority of the time you will never be able to find something as great as something you craft yourself! Try smithing your own armor and throw some enchantments on there. You'd be surprised at what you can make.
